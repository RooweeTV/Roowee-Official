About Me

Roowee

My name is Royeland John Basanes, and I'm 25 years old. I was born on November 26, 1999, in General Luna, Quezon Province. My father was in the army and stationed in Quezon Province, where he met my mother, who worked as a stenographer in the local court and still does to this day. Unfortunately, my father passed away in 2004, which was a difficult time for our family. Despite the challenges, I was determined to continue my education and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, majoring in Web and Mobile Development, in 2023. My interest in technology began in 2015 when I started learning about computers, photo and video editing, and game development. The pandemic in 2020 gave me the opportunity to start freelancing as a graphic designer, and I continued building my skills throughout my college years. During my internship at the university, I was assigned to the Department of the Committee on Publication and Communication Affairs, where I worked as a video editor and graphic designer, completing 486 hours of work. Today, I continue freelancing as a graphic artist, while also pursuing my passion for game development and streaming. I have a basic understanding of programming and web development, which led me to create this website using HTML, Bootstrap, and CSS.
